Description

  • Machines are made to reduce human efforts, but today, Robots are almost capable of replacing humans itself! Robots are modelled using concepts of mechanical engineering, electrical and electronics. Computer science is used to program the robot as per the intended function. Hence Robotics is viewed as a branch closely related to integration of computer science and various engineering concepts.

What You Will Learn?

  • Mechanical engineering concepts- DC motor, Stepper Motor, temperature and humidity sensors, degrees of freedom, Forward and Inverse Kinematics,
  • Computer Engineering aspects -Types and Functions of Arduino, Data Communication with Bluetooth Module, WiFi
  • Building a Line Follower Robot
  • Discussion on Projects.

What You Will Get?

  • Certificate of Completion on successful submission of projects and assignment.
  • Certificate of Participation from Ecell and Verzeo.
